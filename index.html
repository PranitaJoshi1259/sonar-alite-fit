Alite Fit is an outdoor fitness training group that specializes in various fitness programs, including running, corporate wellness and team building, and marathon training.

Alite Fit collaborates with companies to enhance employee engagement programs by offering fitness solutions. Our corporate training programs are designed to transition employees from sedentary lifestyles to active and healthier ones, which in turn leads to improved physical and mental well-being.

Our corporate wellness program is not just about fitness. It also serves as an effective stress-reliever, fosters team building, enhances team management skills, and contributes to a happier and more cohesive work environment. We believe that healthier employees make for a stronger, more motivated team.

Alite Fit's marathon training program offers several benefits, including improved physical fitness, the opportunity to connect with nature, and the chance to meet like-minded individuals.
